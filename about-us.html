About Us
Alban/Lek Import and Export is a dynamic and growing family-owned business that has been making significant strides in the international trade industry since its inception in 2017. Our Head office in Addis Ababa, Alban Import and Export has established itself as a reliable and reputable player in both the import and export markets. Our commitment to quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success. we start our journey with a modest team of five dedicated employees. Over the years, through relentless hard work, strategic planning, and a keen understanding of market dynamics, we have expanded our operations and now proudly employ a team of 20 skilled professionals. Our growth is a testament to our unwavering dedication to excellence and our ability to adapt to the ever-changing global trade landscape. Our primary focus is on the trade of high-quality agricultural products and innovative solutions, catering to diverse market needs both locally and internationally.
